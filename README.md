- **Rule 1 (Coprime Gate):** gcd(k,6) separates left-handed quarks from everything else
- **Rule 2 (mod 3 Gate):** separates neutrinos from charged leptons and right-handed quarks
- **Rule 3 (mod 2 Gate):** separates up-type from down-type among left-handed quarks

## The 144-Clock

The 144-point circle is derived from the symmetry group of three quaternionic loops in Borromean configuration:

**G = 2T × S₃**, where 2T is the binary tetrahedral group (order 24) and S₃ is the permutation group on three objects (order 6).

|G| = 24 × 6 = **144**

The derivation uses standard group theory and the spinor double-cover property of quaternions. No free parameters.
